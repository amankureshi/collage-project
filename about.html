Armanee Tea Shop

A Founded in 2010

Armanee Tea Packer was founded in 2010 by Ayub Khan, the father of Harish Khan. From the very beginning, Harish used to sell tea door-to-door in the village area. The daily profit from selling 2 kgs of tea was enough to support the family. At that time, Harish didn’t have the financial means to expand the business, but his vision was clear. Later, as Harish’s children grew up, two of them, Imran Khan and Farhan Khan, joined the family business and started increasing sales until 1990.
